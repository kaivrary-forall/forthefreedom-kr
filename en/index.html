Message from the Chairman

Defending Liberal Democracy
and Innovative Vision

"Freedom & Innovation Party is dedicated to defending Korea's liberal democratic system and leading an innovative future based on market economy and rule of law."

Our party inherits the spirit of 'Liberty' from President Syngman Rhee, who adopted liberal democracy at Korea's founding, and the spirit of 'Innovation' from President Park Chung-hee, who laid the foundation for industrialization.

We will firmly address election fraud and anti-state activities, establish rule of law, lead the 4th Industrial Revolution, and guarantee unlimited opportunities for youth. As a unified party for all generations, we will rebuild a free Korea and make it a first-class nation.

For a New Korea: 7 Key Policies

A Party Pursuing Justice, Fairness, and Hope

Firmly cutting off privileges, unfairness, irregularities and corruption to realize a society where all are equal before the law

Eradicating Election Fraud

Abolishing early voting, establishing same-day voting and counting to ensure election transparency

Strengthening National Security

Cracking down on anti-state forces, restoring NIS investigation powers, establishing Drug Enforcement Agency

Building Ladder of Hope for Youth

Foundation for Free Unification

Preparing for unified Korea's prosperity with 100,000 unification workers and North Korean education programs

Cultural Prosperity for a Happy Korea

Realizing a cultured nation, strategic K-content export industry for global cultural leadership
